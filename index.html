About Me

Hello! I'm Vernon Longwani a Software Engineering Student at the University Of Zambia, a dedicated web developer with a passion for creating intuitive and responsive user experiences. I discovered my love for coding during my high school days at Chitulika Secondary School when we used to have JETS competetions, where I built a simple HTML page that sparked a journey into the world of web technologies. Since then, I have honed my skills in front-end development, focusing on clean design and efficient code.

I believe that a well-crafted website should not only look good but also be accessible to everyone. This philosophy drives me to stay updated with the latest best practices in semantic HTML, CSS Grid, Flexbox, and progressive enhancement. I enjoy solving complex layout challenges and turning design mockups into pixel-perfect, responsive websites.

Currently, I am expanding my knowledge in JavaScript frameworks like React and exploring back-end technologies to become a more well-rounded developer. My goal is to contribute to meaningful projects that make a difference in people's lives, whether through e-commerce platforms, educational tools, or community-driven applications.

Outside of coding, I am an avid reader and a hobbyist photographer. I love capturing moments and often draw inspiration from the world around me to bring a creative touch to my work. I am excited to collaborate with like-minded individuals and continue growing as a developer.
